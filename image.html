Kittens

Kittens are the cutest that can be! They're so small with their adorable expressions. They have multiple fun facts that many people don't know about such as:

  • When they smell something unfamiliar, they start hissing like snakes!
  • Kittens can have multiple fathers just from one litter!
  • Kittens' pee and poop aren't thrown away like many people think...it gets eaten by the mother!
  • Kittens with shorter hair will open their eyes sooner than the longer ones!
